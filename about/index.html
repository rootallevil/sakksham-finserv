About Sakksham Finserv LLP

Sakksham Finserv LLP is a professionally managed debt recovery firm committed to delivering ethical, efficient, and legally sound recovery solutions across India. With a pan-India presence and deep expertise across multiple sectors, we specialize in helping businesses recover outstanding dues while maintaining client relationships and upholding regulatory compliance.

Founded with a vision to redefine the standards of recovery services in India, Saksham Finserv brings together a team of experienced professionals with strong industry knowledge and a results-driven approach. Our operations are built on the pillars of integrity, transparency, and strategic execution, ensuring that every recovery process is handled with precision and professionalism.

We serve a wide range of clients-from financial institutions and NBFCs to corporates and service providers-offering tailored solutions that align with the unique nature of each case. Whether it's pre-litigation recovery, post-default resolution, or amicable settlement, our team is equipped to manage every stage of the recovery cycle with diligence and care.

At Saksham Finserv, we don't just recover dues-we restore trust, strengthen compliance, and help businesses regain financial balance with confidence.
