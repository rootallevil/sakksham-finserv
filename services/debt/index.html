Turning Your Bad Debts into Profit

At Sakksham Finserv LLP, we provide end-to-end debt collection services that are efficient, compliant, and tailored to every stage of the recovery cycle. Our solutions are designed specifically for banks, NBFCs, and financial institutions, enabling effective engagement across diverse borrower segments and geographies. We manage the entire recovery process, from early follow-ups through tele-calling and field visits to negotiation and resolution support, as needed.

What distinctly sets us apart is our belief that successful recovery is never a one-size-fits-all endeavour. It's strategy, not standardisation that drives meaningful results. Each case is approached with a tailored plan, built around the borrower's profile, repayment behaviour, and stage of delinquency. This customised and practical methodology accelerates recovery timelines while enhancing cash flow and long-term financial stability for our clients.

Our skilled team of recovery professionals is well-versed in client priorities and committed to delivering focused, ethical, and outcome-oriented solutions. Their methodical and respectful approach consistently ensures high recovery rates while preserving productive relationships with both customers and debtors.
