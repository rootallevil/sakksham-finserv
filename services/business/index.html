Business Correspondance

To ensure greater Financial Inclusion and increase the outreach of the BFSI sector, the banks, NBFC and Credit Co-Op Society started giving permits to some agents that will be present at various places/jurisdiction and act as intermediaries in providing some Banking services through the use of the Business Correspondent Model (BC Model). One of the most significant advantages of the BC model is its potential to drive financial inclusion by providing access to banking services to population groups that have traditionally been excluded; by providing banking services through BCs, banks can help to close these gaps and bring more people into the financial system. BC model is its potential to reduce the cost of banks.
