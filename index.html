Who we are

Sakksham Finserv LLP is a leading debt recovery firm in India, offering end-to-end solutions across diverse sectors. With a strong emphasis on legal compliance, ethical recovery practices, and operational efficiency, we specialize in the resolution of overdue debts, non-performing assets (NPAs), and loan defaults. Our structured and strategic approach ensures timely, reliable outcomes, enabling our clients to strengthen financial control and support long-term business sustainability. At Sakksham Finserv, our operations are built on the core principles of integrity, transparency, and consistent performance.

Why Choose Us?

At Sakksham Finserv LLP, we believe that debt recovery is not merely a financial function, it is a matter of trust, timing, and tact. In an environment where reputational risk is as critical as financial exposure, we stand as your dedicated ally in recovering what is owed ethically, discreetly, and effectively.

1

Pan-India Operational Reach

With a robust network across major cities, our services span the length and breadth of India. This expansive footprint enables us to cater to geographically diverse portfolios with consistency, speed, and localised expertise.

2

End-to-End Recovery Expertise

From initial contact through tele-calling, to field recovery, legal escalation, and structured settlements, we manage the full lifecycle of debt recovery. Our comprehensive approach ensures nothing is overlooked, and every effort is directed towards swift, lawful, and meaningful resolution.

3

Strategic Solutions

We recognize that no two portfolios are the same. That's why we design strategies tailored to the nature of your business, the character of your customer base, and the structure of your receivables. Whether it's high-volume collections or complex legal recoveries, our methods are both adaptive and precise.

4

Domain Expertise

Our team brings in-depth knowledge and practical experience across debt recovery, credit risk, legal compliance, and financial negotiations. This expertise allows us to handle complex recovery cases efficiently and offer solutions that align with industry standards and client expectations.

5

Faster Rate of Recovery

We focus on delivering quick and effective debt recovery solutions that help reduce recovery time and improve cash flow. Our dedicated and result-oriented team works around the clock to ensure timely follow-ups and fast action—making the recovery process smooth, responsive, and efficient.

6

Transparency

We keep you informed every step of the way. With consistent and transparent updates on debt recovery, credit collection, and case developments, you gain full insight into our work, ensuring a reliable and trustworthy partnership from start to finish.
